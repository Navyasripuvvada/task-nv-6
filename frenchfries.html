How to Cook

  • step 1:Peel and cut potatoes into thin sticks.
  • Step 2: Wash and soak in water for 20 minutes.
  • Step 3:Dry them completely.
  • Step 4:Heat oil in a pan.
  • Step 5:Fry until golden and crispy.
  • Step 6: Remove and sprinkle salt.
  • Step 7: Serve hot with ketchup.
